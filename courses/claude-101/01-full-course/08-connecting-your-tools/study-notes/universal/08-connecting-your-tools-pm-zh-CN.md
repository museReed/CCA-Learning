## 一句话摘要

Estimated time: 20 minutes Learning objectives By the end of this lesson, you will be able to: Explain what connectors are and why they matter for your work with Claude Navigate the connectors directory and set up your first connection Use connected tools effectively in your conversations with Claude Video Key takeaways Connectors transform Claude from an assistant into an informed collaborator by giving Claude access to the same tools, data, and context that you use every day. Instead of starting every conversation from scratch, Claude can work directly with your actual information. Connectors allow Claude to read information and perform actions on your behalf.
