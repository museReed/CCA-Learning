## One-Liner

Estimated time: 20 minutes Learning objectives By the end of this lesson, you will be able to: Explain what artifacts are and when Claude creates them Share artifacts with colleagues and publish them publicly Troubleshoot common artifact issues What are artifacts? Artifacts are standalone, interactive outputs that Claude creates in a dedicated window alongside your conversation. Instead of getting a long block of code or text buried in the chat, you see your content rendered and ready to use—whether that's a working website, an interactive chart, or a document you can immediately download.
